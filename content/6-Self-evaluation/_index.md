## SELF-ASSESSMENT OF THE INTERNSHIP

Throughout my internship at **BOOTCAMP FIRST CLOUD AI JOURNEY (FCAJ)**, from **April 17, 2026** to **July 10, 2026**, I had the opportunity to learn, practice, and apply the knowledge I gained at university to a real working and learning environment. This period helped me get closer to the process of developing a system on a cloud computing platform, while also giving me a clearer understanding of the work of a Cloud Engineer in a business setting.

During the Bootcamp, my team members and I worked on the **AI Content Generator Platform** project. The project was deployed on Amazon Web Services (AWS), where I took part in gathering requirements, designing the system architecture, and deploying and configuring AWS services such as VPC, EC2, S3, IAM, RDS, Lambda, API Gateway, CloudFront, and CloudWatch. In addition, I contributed to preparing project documentation, designing system architecture diagrams, testing, and finalizing reports as required by the mentors.

Through the project, I significantly improved my knowledge of Cloud Computing, my skills in deploying AWS services, reading technical documentation, designing system architecture according to the AWS Well-Architected Framework, teamwork, progress management, report presentation, and resolving issues that arose during system deployment.

Throughout the internship, I always tried to complete assigned tasks on schedule, proactively researched official AWS documentation whenever I faced difficulties, and actively discussed with mentors and team members to find suitable solutions. Although there is still much more for me to learn, I have consistently maintained a growth mindset and remained open to feedback in order to improve myself.

## Areas for Improvement

Through the internship, I recognize that there are still several areas I need to continue improving in order to better meet the requirements of future work.

* Continue deepening my knowledge of AWS services, particularly system architecture solutions, security, and cost optimization on the Cloud platform.
* Further develop my ability to analyze and resolve issues that arise during system deployment, leading to more effective and optimized solutions.
* Improve communication and idea-presentation skills during team meetings and when reporting to mentors, in order to convey content more clearly and persuasively.
* Enhance time-management and task-organization skills to ensure progress when working on projects with multiple tasks running simultaneously.
* Continue learning new technologies related to Cloud Computing, DevOps, and AI to strengthen professional competence and better meet industry requirements after graduation.

Overall, I consider my internship at BOOTCAMP FIRST CLOUD AI JOURNEY to be a very valuable experience. The program not only helped me strengthen my professional knowledge but also trained me in many practical skills and gave me a clearer direction toward my goal of becoming a Cloud Engineer in the future.
