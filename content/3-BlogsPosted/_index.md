## [Blog 1 - CloudFront Launches Flat-Rate Pricing: No More "Bill Shocks" When Traffic Spikes](3.1-Blog1/)

This blog covers AWS's groundbreaking shift from the risky "pay-as-you-go" billing model to fixed, all-in-one monthly plans for CloudFront. You will discover the details of the 4 new pricing tiers (including a Free plan ideal for startups), the no-overage-fee mechanism, and the cost-exemption perk for malicious traffic (like DDoS). This guide helps you optimize your network infrastructure costs in a safe and predictable way.

## [Blog 2 - [AWS Security] Serverless Security Does Not Rely on a Single Layer](3.2-Blog2/)

This blog tackles the common misconception that "serverless is automatically secure." It dives deep into a defense-in-depth architecture featuring 7 independent protection layers (spanning Edge, Identity, API, Data, and Monitoring). Readers will learn how to design a resilient microservices system, ensuring that if one checkpoint is breached, the subsequent barriers will stand strong to minimize the blast radius.
